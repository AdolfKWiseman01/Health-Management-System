Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer a prescription? ▼

Call us at (123) 456-7890 with your previous pharmacy's details, or bring your prescription bottle. We'll handle the transfer.

Allow 1-2 business days for completion. We'll notify you when ready.

Do you offer medication counseling? ▼

Yes, our pharmacists offer one-on-one sessions to discuss dosage, side effects, interactions, and proper storage.

Request a session when you pick up, or call to schedule. Highly recommended for new or complex regimens.

What insurance plans do you accept? ▼

We accept most major commercial, Medicare Part D, Medicaid, and local/national plans.

Bring your insurance card or call us to confirm coverage and discuss payment options for uninsured patients.

Can I get immunizations? ▼

Yes, our certified pharmacists administer common vaccines like Flu, COVID-19, Tdap, and more.

Appointments are recommended, but walk-ins are often accommodated. Most insurance plans cover routine immunizations.

How can I order prescription refills online? ▼

Refill via the Care Connect Patient Portal or call us directly at (123) 456-7890. Have your prescription number(s) ready.

Do you offer home delivery? ▼

Yes, we offer home delivery for eligible prescriptions within a local radius. Contact us for details on eligibility and fees.
